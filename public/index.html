About Us

Jazz41.com is based out of Columbus, Ohio. Jazz41.com plays smooth Jazz music to uplift you and keep you in the groove. We have put together a playlist that you will enjoy. Get connected with us.

There are a lot of good artists here in the Columbus area that are not getting their true and just exposure, nice jazz clubs, concerts that will come this way with the right outlet to expose their music.

Jazz41.com will be that outlet to give the artists the exposure they need and create that smooth jazz environment that is already here. Just need to be exposed more in Columbus.

There are many outlets to listen to jazz but will not connect you to the jazz community here in Columbus. Sit back and enjoy us. Tell someone about Jazz41.com. Keep up with the latest things going on in Columbus and surrounding areas.
